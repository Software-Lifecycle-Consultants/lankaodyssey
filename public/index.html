Core Rules

Every rider must pass each day’s mandatory checkpoint before cut-off.

Live tracker must stay active throughout the event.

No unauthorized outside support if unsupported rules are enforced.

Riders must obey local traffic laws and emergency protocols.

Missing cut-off, missing checkpoints, or tracker tampering can trigger DQ.

Classification outcomes: Finisher, DNF, DQ.

FAQ

Is this a race?

It is designed as an unsupported odyssey focused on legal completion within official time limits.

Can I choose my own route?

Yes, between mandatory checkpoints. No, regarding the checkpoint locations themselves.

Is live tracking mandatory?

Yes. Every official rider must use an approved live tracker throughout the event.

Can I register instantly?

No. Applications are reviewed before confirmation and payment.
