# Lanka Odyssey Platform Build Brief

## 1) Product summary

**Product name:** Lanka Odyssey  
**Edition:** Ancient Kingdoms Edition  
**Event format:** Unsupported ultra-cycling odyssey with mandatory daily checkpoint deadlines  
**Route concept:** Negombo to Galle, 7 days, target distance around 2000 km  
**Event timing:** Late August  

### Core positioning
A premium ultra-cycling expedition across Sri Lanka inspired by the storytelling structure of iconic odyssey-style events: unsupported, route-flexible between mandatory checkpoints, strict time cut-offs, live rider tracking, and a strong cultural narrative.

### Brand promise
Not a race for podium hunters. An odyssey for serious riders.

### Event truth
Riders must pass each day’s mandatory checkpoint before the daily cut-off. Missing a checkpoint cut-off results in official disqualification.

- Duration: 7 days
- Target course distance: approximately 2000 km
- Theme: Ancient Kingdoms of Sri Lanka
- Rider support model: self-supported, official emergency oversight only

## 16) FAQ page content

### Is this a race?
The event is designed as an unsupported odyssey rather than a classic podium-first road race. The main objective is to complete the course legally and within the official time limits.

### Can I choose my own route?
Yes, between mandatory checkpoints. No, with regard to the checkpoints themselves.

### What happens if I miss a daily cut-off?
You are disqualified from official classification.

### Is live tracking mandatory?
Yes. Every official rider must use an approved tracker throughout the event.

### Can I bring my own tracker?
If the organizer allows approved third-party devices, riders may be eligible for a reduced tracker fee.

### Can I buy transport from Galle to the airport?
Yes. Official paid transfer options will be available during registration and after confirmation.

### Can I register instantly?
No. Applications are reviewed before confirmation.

### Do I need prior ultra experience?
Strongly recommended, and may be required depending on final event policy.

### Will accommodation be provided?
No accommodation is included by default unless specifically stated in a package.

## 18) Payment approach

## Recommended payment architecture
Support two modes:

### Option A: Stripe Checkout
Use Stripe Checkout Sessions for fast, low-friction one-time payments for:
- registration fee
- transfer add-ons
- tracker rental
- jersey and merch
- photo packs

### Option B: Local Sri Lanka gateway fallback or parallel option
If the event business entity is Sri Lankan and direct Stripe merchant onboarding is not available, integrate a Sri Lankan gateway such as PayHere or WEBXPAY as an alternative checkout path.

## Recommended payment flow
1. User applies
2. Admin approves application
3. System generates invoice / payment request
4. User pays via Stripe Checkout or local gateway
5. Webhook confirms payment
6. Rider status changes to Paid
7. Confirmation email sent automatically

## Payment statuses
- Draft
- Awaiting review
- Accepted pending payment
- Paid
- Partially paid
- Refunded
- Cancelled
- Chargeback / payment issue

## Refund logic to build
- refundable before a deadline
- partially refundable after a deadline
- non-refundable tracker or merch after issuance if needed
